Refund Policy

Information about donation refunds

Important: Please review this policy before making a donation.

Donation Refund Policy

All donations to AI World Leaders, Inc. are final.

When Refunds May Be Considered

While donations are generally final, we understand that errors can occur. We will consider refund requests in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate donations made in error
  • Incorrect donation amounts due to technical issues
  • Unauthorized transactions
  • Other exceptional circumstances at our discretion

Refund Request Process

Time Limit

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original donation date.

How to Request

Email us at aiworldleadersinc@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used for the donation
  • Donation amount and date
  • PayPal transaction ID (if available)
  • Reason for the refund request

Review Process

We will review your request within 5-7 business days and respond via email with our decision.

Refund Processing

If approved, refunds will be processed back to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Important Notes

  • Refunds are processed at the sole discretion of AI World Leaders, Inc.
  • Processing fees charged by PayPal are non-refundable
  • Recurring donation cancellations do not automatically trigger refunds for past donations
  • Tax deduction claims should be adjusted if a refund is issued

Alternative to Refunds

If you're unable to receive a refund but would like to support a different cause, please contact us to discuss potential alternatives.
