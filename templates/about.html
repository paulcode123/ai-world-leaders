Our Story

The Spark

It all started with a shared passion for artificial intelligence and a recognition of the incredible potential it holds for transforming education. As students at Bronx Science, both Paul and Rylin were immersed in advanced STEM programs, but they realized that many students around the world don't have access to the same opportunities.

The Connection

Rylin's connection to Cameroon through his father opened our eyes to the specific challenges faced by students in developing countries. We saw an opportunity to bridge the digital divide and bring cutting-edge AI education to communities that could benefit most from technological innovation.

The Mission

Together, we founded AI World Leaders with a clear mission: to democratize access to AI education and empower students in developing countries to become the next generation of innovators and leaders. Our upcoming trip to Cameroon represents the first step in this ambitious journey.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student, regardless of their geographic location or economic circumstances, has access to world-class AI education and the tools they need to innovate.
