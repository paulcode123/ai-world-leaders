Information We Collect

When you make a donation through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, email address
  • Donation Information: Donation amount, donation frequency, optional message
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through PayPal (we do not store payment details)
  • Transaction Information: PayPal transaction ID, donation date and time

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your donation and provide you with a receipt
  • Send you updates about our mission and impact (if you opt-in)

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

Information Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your donor information to third parties. Your personal information is kept confidential and is only used by AI World Leaders, Inc. for the purposes stated above.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes

  • With service providers who help us operate our website (they are bound by confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All payment processing is handled securely through PayPal's encrypted systems.
