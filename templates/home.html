Journey to Cameroon

Guided by a World Bank study demonstrating widespread connectivity gaps, we deploy HP Victus laptops pre-loaded with five locally hosted LLMs. This solution empowers students without reliable internet or personal devices to explore AI independently.

60
Participants
3
Workshop Sessions (3 hrs each)
12
Dedicated Laptops

In Limbe (PC Bobende, Southwest Province), we deliver structured instruction on LLM architecture, capabilities, and ethical considerations. Teen learners (ages 14–18) collaborate on problem-solving projects that address local challenges identified in pre-session surveys. Upon completion, devices remain housed in a secured, internet-free learning lab—where students continue self-paced study via Khan Academy and interact with our offline AI tools under guided supervision.
